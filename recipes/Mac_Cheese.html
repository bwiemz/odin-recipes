How to make Mac and cheese

This is a recipe on how to make Mac and Cheese. Mac and cheese is an easy to make delicous recipe that both adults and kid alike enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 package of elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 block of sharp cheddar shredded
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground red pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400f, boil your pasta and grease a 2qt baking dish.
  2. Microwave your milk for 1 1/2 minutes. Melt your butter in a skillet over medium heat; whisk in the flower until smooth.
  3. Gradually whisk in the warm milk until thickened.
  4. Whisk in the salt, black pepper, 1 cup of cheese and red pepper until smooth.
  5. Stir in your cooked pasta. Pur the pasta mix into your baking dish then top with your remaining cheese.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes or until golden and bubbly.
