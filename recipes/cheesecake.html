How to make Cheesecake

Description

This is a recipe on how to make cheesecake. It's fast simple easy to make and tastes delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups of gram crackers crushed.
  • 3tbsp of sugar
  • 1/3 cup of butter
  • 4 packages of cream cheese
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 tsp of vanilla
  • 4 eggs
  1. Preheat your oven to 325f
  2. Crush your gram crackers then mix them with 3tbsp sugar and butter.
  3. Beat your cream cheese, 1 cup of sugar and the vanilla until blended. Add the eggs, 1 at a time, mixing on low speed. Pour over the crust.
  4. Once the oven is done preheating bake for 55 minutes, or until the center is almost set.
  5. Let it cool before removing from the pan then refrigerate.
